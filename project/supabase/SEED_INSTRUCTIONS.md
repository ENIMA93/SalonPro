# Load Dummy Data (DH Morocco)

To see the dummy data in SalonPro:

1. Open **Supabase Dashboard** → your project
2. Go to **SQL Editor** → **New query**
3. Copy the contents of `seed_dummy_data.sql`
4. Paste and click **Run**
5. Refresh your SalonPro app

The seed includes: 5 staff, 10 services, 5 inventory items, 6 appointments (3 past, 3 future).
